Visualization

With this dataset, I created a VegaLite line plot to show the relationship of the water level in Lake Huron over the years of 1875 to 1972. I also used the built in idyll button function to create two unique buttons. If you click the Water Level button, you are able to see the change in water level by color. If you click the None button, you can revert back to the original VegaLite plot. I find it interesting how the water level does not have a definite linear trend. I think it would be valuable if there were additional columns on rainfall and heat index in these years to understand how the water level is affected in this way. Color Scheme Source
